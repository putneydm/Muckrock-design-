Who we are

Filing Freedom of Information Requests doesn't need to be difficult. At MuckRock, we are dedicated to wading through the muck so our users don't have to.

How it works

MuckRock serves journalists, researchers, activists and historians, with a track record of more than 2,000 requests. Simply login to your account and submit your FOI request via our simple web-interface.
