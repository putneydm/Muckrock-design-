Outdated definitions of news media exclude HuffPo, online-only outlets (including MuckRock!)

by Anais Vaillant

Filing Freedom of Information Requests doesn't need to be difficult. At MuckRock, we are dedicated to wading through the muck so our users don't have to.

What does this mean for you? Less time spent mitigating complex bureaucratic processes so that you can focus on analyzing and reporting on the issues that matter most to you and your organization or business.

As the only public records request service of its kind in the United States, MuckRock serves journalists, researchers, activists and historians, with a track record of over 2,000 requests. Simply login to your account and submit your FOI request via our simple web-interface.
